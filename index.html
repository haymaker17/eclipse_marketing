Bel Powersolutions & Protection

With its focus on Renewable Energy and “Green” power solutions, Bel Power Solutions has established itself as the world's second largest provider of solar inverters.

Bel

Bel and its divisions are primarily engaged in the design, manufacture, and sale of products used in networking, telecommunications, high-speed data transmission, commercial aerospace, military, transportation, and consumer electronics.

Bud Industries

Bud Manufactures an extensive line of affordable products ranges from small metal electronics enclosures to large cabinet racks, products made from plastic as well as metal, and standard electronic products in addition to those that meet NEMA and seismic requirements.

Caplugs

For more than 65 years, Caplugs has been the leader in product protection with innovative plastic molded components that solve industry-wide challenges in many markets such as medical, masking, energy, automotive, electronics, hydraulics and more.

Fastener Specialty

We manufacture high quality nut ring mounting brackets for all military and commercial cylindrical connectors and other Aerospace/Industrial mounting needs.

Hirose

With its focus on Renewable Energy and “Green” power solutions, Power-One has established itself as the world's second largest provider of solar inverters.

Judd Wire

Judd Wire is a high technology wire company, specializing in the use of electron beam crosslinked thermosets for wire insulation.

NIC

NIC Components Corp., in partnership with Nippon Industries Co. Ltd., has been manufacturing and designing passive components for over thirty years. By integrating design, raw materials and production expertise, NIC has established itself as a leader in passive component technology.

Radiall

Since 1952, we have been enabling the future and collaborating with our customers to succeed. We have developed an award winning reputation and established strong relationships with our customers by providing innovative solutions. Signal Transformer

Signal Transformer is celebrating over 50 years of manufacturing transformers, chokes, inductors and custom or modified standard products. Our commitment to quality is noted by its in-house ISO 17025 certified lab that enables us to participate in the CSA’s Supervised Manufacturing Testing & Certification (SMTC) program.

Stewart

The Stewart Connector portfolio of products include one of the broadest ranges of Ethernet connectivity. Our interconnect products includes passive jacks, plugs and cable assemblies that provide connectivity in networking equipment.

TRP

The product portfolio of TRP Connector includes discrete magnetic solutions, but is primarily focused on Integrated Connector Modules (ICM), where the Ethernet magnetics solution is integrated into a connector package.

Unipower

UNIPOWER is a world leading provider of dependable high‐efficiency power electronics, energy conversion systems and power supplies. UNIPOWER solutions include a complete line of high-efficiency rectifiers, inverters and DC power systems for mission critical applications.

About Us

People know Eclipse Marketing Group by the companies we keep. We keep good companies for a very long time. Our greatest assets are the relationships we've built over the years. We've linked the nation's most reputable electronic component manufacturers and distributors with their OEM customers in the Pacific Northwest since 1946.

What sets Eclipse apart? Several company policies allow us to posture as a cohesive team: product managers who are responsible for line performance and training; salaried employees whose profit share and bonus depend upon overall company performance not individual performance. We believe these policies enhance teamwork and promote our Dedication to the industry and to each other.

Our corporate offices are located in Kirkland, WA in the heart of the electronic industry in Washington State. Our Oregon office is located in Beaverton, the center of the "Silicon Forest." Both offices are linked by computer networks and electronic mail, ensuring that principals, customers, distributors and employees all have accurate and timely information. Nine outside sales representatives and four inside support specialists provide complete coverage for the ERA Cascade Chapter territories of Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Western Montana, Alaska and British Columbia.

Our market consists of over 500 active customers in such segments as Aircraft and Aerospace, Medical and Test Instrumentation, Computers and Telecommunications. We know our market and have made a commitment to our customers to bring them the best possible solutions to their most technologically challenging opportunities. Whether it be surface mount processing or RFI shielding of cable assemblies, Eclipse will always be there first with the most innovative solutions.

As our name implies, Eclipse will always "Surpass Existing Boundaries." We'd like to put our energies into competing in the marketplace for you. Please contact us today to find out how Eclipse Marketing Group can be of service to your company.
